### Section 2: Your Experience (Open-Ended)
*Ensure these are set to "Paragraph" text fields in your form so users can write freely.*

**Q4. The "Stuck" Feeling**
Describe a time when you felt like Spotify's recommendations were stuck in a loop or a "genre bubble". How did it impact your listening experience?

**Q5. The Workaround**
When Spotify fails to recommend good new music, what is your backup method for finding new artists? (e.g., YouTube, TikTok, specific blogs, asking friends). Walk me through your process.

**Q6. Skip Fatigue**
What is your biggest frustration when using algorithmic playlists like 'Discover Weekly' or 'Release Radar'? 

**Q7. The Magic Wand**
If you could change or add *one thing* to Spotify to make finding new music easier and more accurate for you, what would it be?
